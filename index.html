My Platform

The best quality I bring to the City Council is my strong voice. I am not afraid to stand up for what is right even if I must stand alone. I am not afraid to say what needs to be said even if some may consider it offensive that I disagree with them. I will not simply vote to move things along, but I will carefully consider all matters brought before me, and I will vote for what is best for all citizens of Nephi. I will represent young families, citizens who just moved in, and those who love our small town just the way it is. I want to keep Nephiâ€™s small-town community and the benefits that come with that while doing what is best for all citizens of Nephi.

I have attended city council regularly since I moved to Nephi in April of 2021. I have noticed that it is rare to see members of the council disagree with or even question those who present them with information. I do not think disagreeing has value in itself, but I think several members of our city council are too passive. We need representatives that will carefully consider, learn about, and have an opinion on matters brought before them.

Elect a representative who takes time to learn and carefully consider matters.

Elect a representative who is willing to speak up and speak out.

Elect a strong voice for Nephi.

Elect Vanessa Goates.
